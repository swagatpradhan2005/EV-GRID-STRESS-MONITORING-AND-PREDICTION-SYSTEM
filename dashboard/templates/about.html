Understanding EV Grid Stress Monitoring and Prediction

🎯 Project Overview

The EV Grid Stress Monitoring and Prediction System is an advanced machine learning solution designed to predict electrical grid stress caused by EV charging stations and determine overload risk across Indian states.

This system integrates multiple data sources including:

  • ✓ Real-time EV charging data
  • ✓ Electrical grid metrics (voltage, frequency, load)
  • ✓ Infrastructure capacity information
  • ✓ Renewable energy generation data
  • ✓ EV adoption trends

⚠️ Risk Levels & Mitigation

LOW RISK

Status: Grid is stable and operating within safe parameters.

Action: Continue monitoring and maintain current charging distribution.

MEDIUM RISK

Status: Grid is approaching operational limits.

Action: Implement preventive measures like load shifting and smart charging.

HIGH RISK

Status: Grid is at critical stress level.

Action: Immediate intervention required to prevent outages.

📖 How to Use This System

Step 1: Select Your State

Choose the Indian state for which you want to predict grid stress.

Step 2: Adjust Parameters

Modify EV charging parameters, grid metrics, and infrastructure capacity based on your scenario.

Step 3: Get Prediction

The system predicts grid load (MW) and overload risk level.

Step 4: Review Recommendations

Implement recommended mitigation strategies based on risk level.

💡 Tips for Better Predictions

  • Adjust charging sessions to simulate different demand scenarios
  • Compare results across different times (peak vs off-peak)
  • Test the impact of increasing renewable energy capacity
  • Monitor transformer load - critical threshold is 90%
